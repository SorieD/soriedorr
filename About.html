A little about me

I live in North Carolina with my husband of 31 years and our cat Hazel. Six years ago, we purchased a little land, half of which is wooded, and have been enjoying the peace and quite of rural America.

Currently I work part time as a nurse. I have been a nurse for 31 years. Half at the bedside and half as a Corporate Educator. After I retired/quit my full time job, I decided to go back to the bedside.

Hobbies, wells let's just say I like to keep busy. I plan to share my hobbies with you on this site.
